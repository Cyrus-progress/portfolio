About

I like taking on hard problems from first principles — whether that's redesigning how mRNA vaccines survive without a freezer, or modeling the unit economics of a handmade product. My background spans wet-lab-adjacent research, computational modeling, and shipping real software.

Past projects include CRISPR-edited vitamin-A rice and a succinic-acid biomanufacturing attempt. When I'm not researching or coding, I'm on the ice training as a short-track speed skater.
